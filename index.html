About Me

My name is Emily McGovern. I am currently living in Vicenza Italy, but I am from the United States. I am always on the move, so I don't really have a place called home. However, I love to call my family home. I love playing the violin and piano, video games, videography, traveling, and hanging around with my family. Some of my top favorite countries I have visited are Greece, England, France, Slovenia, and Poland. Some of my goal countries to visited are Norway, Iceland, and Spain.

3 Favorite Countries

France

France is a beautiful country. Even though Paris is insipiring and breath taking, the country side of France is what is most special about this country. In the country side you are able to see beautiful castles and quaint little villages that have a special place in my heart. One city that is a must see is Colmar. It feels like you are stepping into the fairytale story of Beauty and the Beast.

Slovenia

Slovenia is a country full of greenery and magnificent scenery. Even though I have only visited Slovenia a couple of times, it has a special place in my heart. This country has wonderful hikes and breath taking via ferratas. My favorite place to visit is Lake Bled. The city of Bled does not only have a castle over looking a lake, but it has an island in the middle of the lake that contains a beautiful church and a delicious gelato shop.

England

England is always full of wonderful and enjoyment. If you don't fall in love with the accents, you sure will fall in love with what the country has to offer. To it's quaint country side to the big city of London, this country has it all. I especially love learning and seeing the history. The historical sights are fascinating to see and learn about throughout London.
