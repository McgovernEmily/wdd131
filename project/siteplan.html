Overview

Site Name

Frenchy Travel Suggestions

Domain Name

traveling-french-cities.org

Purpose

Have you ever wanted to travel to a beautiful country full of wonder and culture, but need help knowing where to book everything or where to start? This beautiful French traveling website will display cozy hotels, delicious restaurants, and fun-filled activities. It will be an easy way for travelers to get ideas of what to do while they travel around France.

Audience

  • Travelers: People wanting to plan a visit to France with the idea of leisure and wanting to learn about the culture. They might be looking for information about activities, restaurants, and hotels.
  • Travelers: People who are traveling to France or studying there and want to learn more about what the country has to offer.
  • Family/couples: whether it be families or couples living in France or traveling to France, these hotels, restaurants, and activities will be available for all.

Scenarios

  • “What are the top 3 must-visit attractions in Paris and what are their opening hours?”
  • “I have a business meeting in Paris next week. What are some quiet cafes or restaurants where I can have a business lunch?
  • “I am studying abroad in Italy and want to travel to Colmar. What are some cheap hotels?”
  • “What are some activities that are fun for both adults and children?”
