Paris

Paris is the beautiful capital of France. It is one of the most important and influential cities in the world. With it's fashion, literature, art, and architecture to keep you looking in awe. Paris is full of emotions and there is always something to see, do, and appreciate.

Paris Hotels

  • Hotel Paradis: Ranging from anywhere between $120-180 depending on room size, 18 minute metro ride to Louvre
  • Hotel Royal Madeleine: Ranging from anywhere between $160-180 per night, 15 minute metro ride to Arc de Triomphe
  • Hotel Adele and Jules: Ranging from under $200 per night, 18 minute metro ride to Louvre

Paris Restaurants/Cafes

  • Au Pied de Cochon: Full of friendly and welcoming staff, is a bit pricy, but serves the best French onion soup
  • Jozi Cafe: One of the top cafes in paris, with a beautiful view of the Pantheon. Pastries and coffee is divine.
  • Little Breizh: A cute and charming shop full of delicious savory and sweet crepes

Paris Activities/Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower: Walk all the way to the top of the Famous Eiffel Tower
  • The Louvre Museum: Find the most historic and beautiful art, with the famous Mona Lisa painting
  • Palace of Versailles: Walk through the beautiful garden and the breathtaking palace of Versailles
