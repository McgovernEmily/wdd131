French Culture

Bonjour! French culture is full of excitement and beauty. French culture is most commonly associated with Paris, which is a center of fashion, cuisine, art, and architecture, but life outside of the City of Lights is a very different and can vary by region. Overall the French culture includes art and architecture, cuisine and wine, fashion, language and literature, music and dance, sports, history, and heritage.

French Beauty

France if full of beauty, from stunning cities like Paris to the gorgeous wineries in the French countryside, and the Mediterranean beauty of southern France. This country has tons to offer with it's glorious rolling vineyards, canyons, majestic villages, and architecture. Some of the most breathtaking places to visit while traveling along France include Palace of Versailles, Eiffel Tower, Chateaux of the Loire Valley, and Chateau de Chenonceau.
